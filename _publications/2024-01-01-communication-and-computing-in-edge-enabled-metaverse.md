abstract_text: |-
  To enable ubiquitous, seamless, and embodied access to the Metaverse, we discuss the
  communication and networking challenges and cutting-edge solutions and concepts that
  leverage next-generation communication systems for users to immerse in and interact with
  embodied avatars in the Metaverse. Moreover, given the high computation costs required,
  e.g., to render 3D virtual worlds and run data-hungry artificial intelligence-driven
  avatars, we discuss the computation challenges and cloud–edge–end computation framework-
  driven solutions to realize the Metaverse on resource-constrained edge devices.
